# Data Sharing Agreements: The Foundation for a Research Partnership
Repository for data sharing agreements and white papers created by the Sitka Sound Science Center for partners in research and education.

### Contact
For questions about our data sharing agreements or to collaborate on shared infrastructure, please contact: research@sitkascience.org.

### Research Data Statement
At the Sitka Sound Science Center (SSSC), we are committed to the principles of Open Science to promote transparency, reproducibility, equity, and collaboration in scientific research. As a place-based research institution in Southeast Alaska, we recognize our responsibility to manage data in ways that are both scientifically sound and culturally respectful.
For more info see our repository on our company research data policies: [https://github.com/Sitka-Sound-Science-Center/research_data_statement]
